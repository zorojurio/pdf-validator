PDF Validation

PDF validation is the process of ensuring that a PDF document complies with certain standards or criteria, such as format specifications or digital signatures. It is important because:

  • It helps ensure the integrity and authenticity of the document, making it reliable for legal and official purposes.
  • It prevents tampering or unauthorized modifications to the document, preserving its original content.
  • It enhances document security by verifying digital signatures and encryption, if applicable.
  • It ensures compatibility with different software and devices, as compliant PDFs are more likely to render correctly across platforms.
